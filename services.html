Mining Supplies & PPE

Compliant safety equipment and essential site consumables

Comprehensive Supply Solutions

We supply a wide range of essential mine and site consumables that meet industry safety standards and operational requirements. All our products are sourced from reputable manufacturers and comply with South African safety regulations.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

Complete range of safety gear including helmets, goggles, ear protection, respirators, and fall protection equipment for mining and industrial applications.

Safety Footwear & Workwear

Industrial-grade gumboots, safety shoes, protective clothing, hi-vis vests, and specialized workwear for various mining environments.

Site Consumables

Essential supplies including safety signage, barriers, first aid kits, fire extinguishers, and general site maintenance materials for mining operations.

Quality Assurance

All products are certified and regularly tested to ensure they meet the highest safety standards and regulatory requirements for the mining industry.

  • Scheduled deliveries to maintain inventory
