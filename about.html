About Us

Delivering excellence in mining supplies, PPE, equipment rentals and logistics

Our Story

Established in 2026, Sarah M Projects (Pty) Ltd is a dynamic and forward-thinking company dedicated to providing exceptional operational support to South Africa's mining, construction, and industrial sectors. Founded by industry professionals with extensive experience in mining operations and supply chain management, our company was built on the understanding that successful projects depend on trusted partnerships and dependable supply chains.

At Sarah M Projects (Pty) Ltd, we specialize in mining supplies, PPE, logistics, and equipment rentals, ensuring that our clients have access to the best resources required to keep their operations running smoothly and efficiently. We are committed to providing top-quality Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) and essential industrial supplies that meet industry safety standards, helping organisations maintain safe and compliant work environments.

Guided by our core values of safety, reliability, and customer-centric service, we focus on building strong and lasting partnerships with our clients. By taking a collaborative approach, we carefully understand each client's unique operational needs and deliver tailored solutions that enhance productivity, improve efficiency, and contribute to the overall success of their projects.

Today, we serve clients across all nine South African provinces, from major mining operations in the Northern Cape to construction projects in Gauteng and industrial developments in Mpumalanga. Our growth is driven by our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction. As a 100% South African owned and managed company, we understand the local landscape, regulations, and challenges. We're proud to contribute to the success of projects that drive our nation's economy forward.

Established under the leadership and vision of Ntokozo Maboa, Sarah M Projects (Pty) Ltd reflects a strong commitment to excellence, innovation, and dependable service in supporting industry growth. Ntokozo brings years of practical experience in mining operations and supply chain management, ensuring that our services are grounded in real-world understanding of client needs.
